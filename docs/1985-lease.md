## 1985 lease for 24 Queens Road (all text)

**NOW THIS DEED WITNESSETH as follows:-**

1. IN CONSIDERATION of the rent and the Lessee's covenants
hereinafter contained and reserved the Lessor HEREBY DEMISES unto the Lessee ALL THAT the property described in the First Schedule hereto (hereinafter called "the Upper Flat") subject to the rights contained in the Third Schedule hereto EXCEPT AND RESERVING out of this Lease for the benefit of the property described in the Second Schedule hereto (hereinafter called "the Lower Flat") the rights set forth in the Fourth Schedule hereto TO HOLD the Upper Flat except and reserved as aforesaid unto the Lessee for a term of 999 years fran the date hereof at the rent of one peppercorn subject to the covenants conditions and stipulations hereinafter contained
2. THE Lessee to the intent and so as to bind the Upper Flat and so as to benefit and protect the Lower Flat and each and every part thereof HEREBY COVENANTS with the Lessor and her successors in title that the Lessee and the persons deriving title under him will at all times hereafter observe and perform the stipulations and obligations set out in the Fifth Schedule hereto
3. THE Lessor to the intent and so as to bind the Lower Flat and to benefit aril protect the Upper Flat and each and every part thereof HEREBY COVENANTS with the Lessee that the Lessor and the persons deriving title under her will at all times hereafter observe and perform the stipulations and obligations set out in the Sixth Schedule hereto
4. IT IS HEREBY CERTIFIED that the transaction hereby effected does not form part of a larger transaction or of a series of transactions in respect of which the amount or value or the aggregate amount or value of the consideration exceeds £30,000.00 IN WITNESS whereof the parties hereto have hereunto set their
hands and seals the day and year first before written
